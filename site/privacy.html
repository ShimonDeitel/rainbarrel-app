Privacy Policy

Rainbarrel is an offline-first app. All data you enter (location, lastCleaned, diverterOK, notes) is stored locally on your device in the app's Application Support directory and is never transmitted to any server.

Rainbarrel does not collect, track, or share any personal data. The app uses Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases, which is handled entirely by Apple; we never see your payment information.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed with it.
